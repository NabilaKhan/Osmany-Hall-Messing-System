ABOUT US

At Osmany Hall Messing System, we target the day to day problem of students related to meal and other services in Osmany Hall. This website will help to solve the problems and hazards of maintaining the hall services manually. Students can easily sign their meal using the system and also can see the food bill and mess bill whenever they want. Moreover, students can pay bill through the website.

"How can I contact you if I have a complaint or need any help?"

You can contact us by submitting a feedback or suggesting any edit at: https://www.website.com/osmanihall/ or you can inform us in our official facebook page or contact us in the given email address and contact numbers below We will respond to your problems within 48 hours. If you are not satisfied with the outcome of your complaint, you can escalate it by providing additional details in your feedback and complaint.
